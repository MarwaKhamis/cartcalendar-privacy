🎁 CartCalendar Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CartCalendar. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

CartCalendar collects the following information:

  • Gift events you create (names, dates, categories)
  • Your country and currency preference
  • App usage data to improve the experience

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Any sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Display your gift events and reminders
  • Show relevant gift deals based on your preferences
  • Send you notifications about upcoming events
  • Improve the app experience

4. Data Storage

All your event data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on external servers. Your data stays on your phone.

5. Affiliate Links

CartCalendar uses affiliate links to retailers such as Amazon, eBay, Kmart, Target, and others. When you tap "View deal" and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

6. Third Party Services

Our app uses the following third party services:

  • Expo (app framework)
  • React Native (mobile framework)

7. Children's Privacy

CartCalendar is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
